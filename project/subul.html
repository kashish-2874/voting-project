SUBUL TOUQEER

Sumbul Touqeer rose to fame with Imlie where she played the titular role and was seen with Fahmaan Khan. Their jodi was adored by the audience but a leap in the daily soap forced the two actors to leave the show in August this year. Beside Imlie, Sumbul has done popular TV shows like Jodha Akbar, Waaris and Ishaaron Ishaaron Mein among others.
