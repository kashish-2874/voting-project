TINA DUTTA

Uttaran fame Tina Datta is another confirmed contestant. The actress, who started her career on television with Sister Nivedita in 1996, is quite popular on social media where she posts some of her most glamorous photos. Besides Uttaran, some of the other popular shows that Tina has done include Karmaphal Daata Shani, Khatron Ke Khiladi 7 and Daayan.
